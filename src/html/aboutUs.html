Our purpose



Throughout our history, Safe Guard has been focused on providing protection, security, and continuity for Sri Lankans and their loved ones in all corners of the island. Over the years, we have grown our business from our humble beginnings as a small, local Life Insurance company, into a progressive and future-ready entity.

Today, repositioned as Safe Guard Life, our business is ready to deliver on a greater calling – to ensure that we uplift the lives and empower the dreams of the millions of Sri Lankans who we reach directly as customers, or touch indirectly by providing the warmth of protection to their families and loved ones, or impact in numerous ways as our employees, partners, and other stakeholders.

Our corporate purpose is a true reflection of the creative vision and pioneering spirit that brought to life the Janashakthi story, and has today become the fuel that drives the entire Safe Guard Life family to success.

Our History



From a small workspace in Muttiah Road was born an Insurance company which would eventually become one of Sri Lanka’s most trusted Insurers.
From the seeds that were sewn by the 25 people that were employed at the time, grew a company that heralded industry firsts and won the hearts of many Sri Lankans across the island.
