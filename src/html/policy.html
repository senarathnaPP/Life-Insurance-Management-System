Policy options


The following options can be included in your policy, subject to underwriting. Each additional option you choose will increase your total premium. If these options are included in your policy, the optional benefits section of your policy schedule will confirm this.
Indexation option
If you have chosen the indexation option, we will review the benefits at the anniversary date in line with the Retail Prices Index calculated 12 weeks before the anniversary date. If you have chosen level cover, then following the review your sum insured will increase in line with the increase in the Retail Prices Index over the 12 month period to the review date. Your premium will increase based on a rate of 1.4 times the percentage increase in the Retail Prices Index. The premium will not increase by more than 14% and will not reduce in the event of the Retail Prices Index being negative. If you have chosen family decreasing cover then following the review your sum insured will increase in line with the increase in the Retail Prices Index over the 12 month period to the review date, but your premium will remain the same. When we review the benefits: n If the Retail Prices Index increase is greater than 0% and is 10% or less, indexation will be applied to the benefits. n If the Retail Prices Index is over 10% we will only increase the benefits up to a maximum of 10%. n If the Retail Prices Index is negative there will be no reduction to the benefits. We will advise you of any change to the benefits, and premium for level cover, 8 weeks before the anniversary date. If you have level cover, if we do not receive confirmation from you that indexation is not required then the change will be applied on the anniversary date. If you wish to cancel indexation before the increase takes effect then you will need to contact us before the anniversary date. If you do not contact us before the anniversary date we will still permit you to cancel indexation up to 30 days after the anniversary date and if necessary we will either refund the increase in premium or adjust future collections. If you have family decreasing cover then it will not be possible to cancel or remove indexation from your policy.

Policy flexibility


Once we have started cover under your policy, you can request that certain changes be made to it. The following amendments will result in us amending your existing policy and will not require any further underwriting.
  • Decreasing the sum insured.
  • Decreasing the policy term.
  • Removing the indexation option on level cover.
  • Removing the waiver of premium option.
You may request these changes be made to your policy with effect from the date that the next premium is due. Where we are asked to remove an option we will simply deduct the charge for that option from the premium. For all other changes we will use the premium rates and age applicable at the time that we make the change. Your premiums following the change cannot be less than the minimum premium of Rs.1400 per month. These policy terms and conditions will continue to apply. The following amendments will require your existing policy to be cancelled and be replaced by a new policy and may require further underwriting:
  • Increasing the policy term.
  • Changing from level cover to decreasing cover.
  • Changing from decreasing cover to level cover.
  • Adding a life insured.
  • Removing a life insured.
You may request these changes be made to your policy with effect from the date that the next premium is due. We will use the premium rates and age applicable at the time that we make the change. New policy terms and conditions applicable at that time will be issued in place of these policy terms and conditions
